Boot the Mac into Recovery Mode (hold down command+R during startup or hold power (M-Series)).
Go to the Utilities menu and open Terminal and type: csrutil disable. This will disable SIP (System Integrity Protection).
Reboot into the OS.
Open the integrated terminal and type two commands:
sudo -s
/bin/rm -rf /var/db/ConfigurationProfiles/Store/*
Shut down
Boot the Mac into Recovery Mode  (hold down command+R during startup or hold power (M-Series)).
Go to the Utilities menu and open Terminal and type: csrutil enable. This will re-enable SIP.
Reboot into the OS.
